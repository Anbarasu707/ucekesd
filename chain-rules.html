Problems On Chain Rule!

Basic Formulas:

  1. Direct Proportion:

    Two quantities are said to be directly proportional, if on the increase (or decrease) of the one, the other increases (or decreases) to the same extent.

    Eg. Cost is directly proportional to the number of articles.
          (More Articles, More Cost)

  2. Indirect Proportion:

    Two quantities are said to be indirectly proportional, if on the increase of the one, the orther decreases to the same extent and vice-versa.

    Eg. The time taken by a car is covering a certain distance is inversely proportional to the speed of the car. (More speed, Less is the time taken to cover a distance.)

    Note: In solving problems by chain rule, we compare every item with the term to be found out.

General Questions:

1. 3 pumps, working 8 hours a day, can empty a tank in 2 days. How many hours a day must 4 pumps work to empty the tank in 1 day?


  
  

View answer

  • Ans: (D)

    • Explanation:


    Let the required number of working hours per day be x.

    More pumps, Less working hours per day (Indirect Proportion)

    Less days, More working hours per day (Indirect Proportion)

    Pumps 4 : 3 :: 8 : x
    Days 1 : 2

    4 x 1 x x = 3 x 2 x 8

    x = (3 x 2 x 8)
    (4)

    x = 12.

2. Running at the same constant rate, 6 identical machines can produce a total of 270 bottles per minute. At this rate, how many bottles could 10 such machines produce in 4 minutes?


  
  

View answer

  • Ans: (D)

    • Explanation


    Let the required number of bottles be x.

    More machines, More bottles (Direct Proportion)

    More minutes, More bottles (Direct Proportion)

    Machines 6 : 10 :: 270 : x
    Time (in minutes) 1 : 4

    6 x 1 x x = 10 x 4 x 270

    x = (10 x 4 x 270)
    (6)

    x = 1800.

3. A fort had provision of food for 150 men for 45 days. After 10 days, 25 men left the fort. The number of days for which the remaining food will last, is:

  
  

View answer

  • Ans: (C)

    • Explanation:


    After 10 days : 150 men had food for 35 days.

    Suppose 125 men had food for x days.

    Now, Less men, More days (Indirect Proportion)

    125 : 150 :: 35 : x       125 x x = 150 x 35

    x = 150 x 35
    125

    x = 42.
